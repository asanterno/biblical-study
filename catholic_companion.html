How to Pray the Rosary

The Rosary is a meditative prayer using beads as a guide. You pray a sequence of prayers while reflecting on key events called Mysteries from the lives of Jesus and Mary. The repeated prayers quiet the mind, freeing you to reflect on each Mystery.

A full Rosary takes about 15–20 minutes. Beginners can start with just one Mystery — about 3–4 minutes. There is no wrong way to begin.

The Order of Prayer

1
Begin — Make the Sign of the Cross, then pray the Apostles' Creed on the crucifix
2
Opening beads — Pray 1 Our Father, then 3 Hail Marys, then 1 Glory Be
3
Announce the Mystery — Say which Mystery you are meditating on. Pause briefly to picture the scene.
4
One Decade — Pray 1 Our Father + 10 Hail Marys + 1 Glory Be. Repeat for all 5 Mysteries.
5
Close — Pray the Hail Holy Queen, then make the Sign of the Cross
